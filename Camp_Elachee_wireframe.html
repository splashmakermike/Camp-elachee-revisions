Section 06 — A Day at Camp · Builds trust / removes anxiety for first-time parents
S6 · DAY AT CAMP
A Typical Day
Your Child's Day, Hour by Hour
🌊 Creek Days: Thursday and Friday Every Week
  • Bring a towel and closed-toe water shoes
  • Wear creek outfit under clothes — no changing on site
  • No swimming ability required
  • All creek activities fully supervised
8:15 AM
Drop-Off & Morning Arrival

Drop-off begins at 8:15. Campers check in with their group leader and settle into the morning with a light activity.

9:00 AM
Morning Adventure Block

Trail hikes, habitat exploration, hands-on science and theme-driven activities led by our naturalist staff.

11:30 AM
Lunch & Free Play

Campers eat the lunch they brought from home and enjoy some unstructured outdoor time. Real rest. Real fun.

12:30 PM
Afternoon Activity Block

Archery, nature crafts, group games, wildlife encounters or creek exploration on Thursdays and Fridays.

2:15 PM
Wrap-Up & Debrief

Groups come together to share what they found. Campers gather their gear. Usually already talking about tomorrow.

2:45 PM
Pick-Up

Pick-up begins at 2:45. Please plan to arrive on time so every camper gets a smooth, happy send-off.

S9 · PRACTICAL INFO
Good to Know
Everything You Need Before You Register

We want you to feel completely prepared before your child's first day — not scrambling for answers on a Monday morning. Here's everything you need to know, right up front.

⏰ Hours & Schedule
  • Drop-off begins8:15 AM
  • Pick-up begins2:45 PM
  • Camp daysMonday–Friday only
  • Before/after careNot available at this time
  • Early pick-upMust be by 2:00 PM
  • Late pick-upConsistent late pick-up may incur a fee. We appreciate your punctuality.
👧 Who Can Attend
  • Elementary campMust be 5+ by Sept 1, 2026 · Entering K–6th
  • Specialty campsEntering grades 4–8
  • Prior experienceNot required
  • SwimmingNot required for creek
  • Special needsContact Camp Director
🎒 What to Pack Every Day
  • Water bottleLabeled, easy to carry
  • LunchIn one lunchbox camper can carry
  • SnacksAt least 2 per day
  • HatOptional but recommended
  • Sunscreen/bugsProvided by Elachee
🥜 Nut-free environment. Please do not pack items containing nuts.
🌊 Creek Day Extra Packing
  • Creek daysEvery Thursday & Friday
  • TowelFull-size towel required
  • Water shoesClosed-toe only
