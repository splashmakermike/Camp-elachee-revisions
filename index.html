A Typical Day

Your Child's Day, Hour by Hour

No mystery. No anxiety. Just a well-planned, adventure-packed day that gives your child the freedom to explore. You have full peace of mind about what's happening every step of the way.

Every day is a little different depending on the theme and the discoveries that find us. But here's the shape of a great camp day at Elachee.

🏊 Creek Days: Thursday and Friday Every Week

Every Thursday and Friday, campers explore Chicopee Creek. It is one of the most loved parts of the whole summer. Water in most areas stays ankle-deep. No swimming ability needed. Just good shoes and a sense of adventure.

  • Bring a towel and closed-toe water shoes
  • Wear your creek outfit under your clothes. No changing facilities on site.
  • Your child does not need to know how to swim
  • Staff supervise all creek activities throughout
8:15 AM
Drop-Off & Morning Arrival
Drop-off begins at 8:15am. Campers check in with their group leader and ease into the morning with light activities and introductions.
9:00 AM
Morning Adventure Block
The heart of the day. Trail hikes, habitat exploration, hands-on science and theme-driven activities led by our naturalist staff.
11:30 AM
Lunch & Free Play
Campers eat the lunch they brought from home (nut-free environment), rest, and enjoy unstructured outdoor play time. Real downtime. Real fun.
12:30 PM
Afternoon Activity Block
Archery, nature crafts, group games, wildlife encounters or creek exploration on Thursdays and Fridays. Every afternoon ties back to the week's theme.
2:15 PM
Wrap-Up & Debrief
Groups gather to share what they discovered. Campers collect their gear and prepare for pick-up. Usually already planning what they'll tell you on the drive home.
2:45 PM
Pick-Up
Pick-up begins at 2:45. Please plan to arrive on time so every camper gets a smooth, happy send-off.

Good to Know

Everything You Need Before You Register

We want you to feel completely prepared before your child's first day. Not scrambling for answers on a Monday morning. Here's everything you need to know, right up front.

⏰Hours & Schedule

  • Drop-off begins 8:15 AM (please do not arrive before 8:15)
  • Pick-up begins 2:45 PM
  • Camp days Monday through Friday only
  • Before/after care Not available at this time
  • Early pick-up Must be by 2:00 PM. No pick-up between 2:00–2:45 PM.
  • Late pick-up Consistent late pick-up may incur a fee. We appreciate your punctuality.

👧Who Can Attend

  • Elementary camp Must be 5 years old on or before September 1, 2026 (entering K–6th grade)
  • Specialty camps Entering grades 4–8
  • Prior experience Not required. All skill levels welcome.
  • Swimming ability Not required for creek activities
  • Special needs Contact Camp Director to discuss options

🎒What to Pack Every Day

  • Water bottle Labeled, easy to carry independently
  • Lunch Packed in one lunchbox your child can carry
  • Snacks At least 2. Camp burns a lot of energy!
  • Hat Optional but highly recommended
  • Sunscreen & bug spray Provided by Elachee. Bring your own if your child has specific needs.
  • Extra items Storage is limited. Pack light so your child can carry their own bag.
🥜 Nut-free environment. Please do not pack any items containing nuts, including peanut butter. This protects all campers.

🌊Creek Day Extra Packing

  • Creek days Every Thursday and Friday
  • Towel Bring a full-size towel
  • Water shoes Closed-toe only (no flip flops)
  • Wear it in Dress in creek clothes under regular clothes. No changing facilities on site.
  • Water depth Ankle-deep in most areas
  • Swimming Not required. All creek time is supervised.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Else You're Wondering

Answers to the questions parents ask most.

Elementary Camp is open to children who will be at least 5 years old on or before September 1, 2026, and are entering Kindergarten through 6th grade. Specialty Camps (Rooted in Nature and Expedition Elachee) are for students entering grades 4–8.
Drop-off begins at 8:15 AM and pick-up begins at 2:45 PM, Monday through Friday. Please do not arrive before 8:15 AM. If you need to pick up early, it must be by 2:00 PM. We cannot accommodate early pick-up between 2:00–2:45 PM as this disrupts group activities. Before and after care is not available at this time.
Not at all. Camp Elachee welcomes children at every comfort level with the outdoors, from city kids who've never hiked a trail to little naturalists who can already identify a dozen bird calls. Our naturalists meet every child exactly where they are.
No swimming ability is required. Water in most areas of Chicopee Creek is no higher than ankle-deep, and all creek activities are fully supervised by staff. Campers should wear closed-toe water shoes and bring a towel, and should arrive wearing their creek clothes since there are no changing facilities on site.
Elachee Family Adventurer Members receive early access beginning February 3, 2026 at 10:00 AM. General public registration opens March 3, 2026 at 10:00 AM. Registration for each week closes when it reaches capacity or the Monday before that week begins. If a week is full, a waitlist option is available.
Yes! Elachee Family Adventurer Members receive a 10% discount on all registrations. Discovery Plus Members receive 20%. Families registering multiple children at the same time receive a 5% sibling discount. Use the contact form on our website to request your sibling discount code. Discounts cannot be combined.
Refunds are available until the cancellation deadline (please confirm current date with the Camp Director as dates vary year to year). After the deadline, refunds require a doctor's note. Week transfers are permitted as space allows. Requests must be made at least 10 business days before the start of the new camp week.
Unfortunately we are not able to accommodate friend group requests. Our groupings are based on age and grade to maintain appropriate staffing structures and activity levels. However, campers who are close in age are often in the same group or participate in joint activities throughout the day.
Campers should bring: a labeled water bottle, lunch packed in a single lunchbox they can carry independently, at least 2 snacks (camp is active and they'll be hungry!), and an optional hat. Elachee provides sunscreen and bug spray. If your child has specific needs, contact Sam directly. Storage is limited, so pack light.
Yes. Camp Elachee is a nut-free environment to protect the safety of all campers. Please do not pack any food items that contain nuts, including peanut butter, nut-based granola bars, or trail mix with nuts. If you have questions about specific foods, contact the Camp Director.
We work hard to make our camp atmosphere as inclusive as possible and want every child to have the best experience. If your child has special needs, a medical condition, or requires any specific accommodations, please reach out through our contact page before registering so we can discuss options together.
Yes! You'll receive a weekly camp newsletter via email on the Monday of your child's camp week. The newsletter includes highlights of what's planned, helpful reminders (especially for Creek Day), and information to help you connect with your camper about their experiences. We encourage you to use it as a conversation starter at pick-up.
Our staff are trained to recognize and respond to children who are having a hard time. We have protocols for homesickness, anxiety, and conflict between campers. If your child is experiencing ongoing difficulty, a staff member will contact you. We never want any child to have a miserable experience. We are good at turning a tough start into a great week.
We are not hosting a Junior CIT or CIT program for the 2026 season. Older campers who have aged out of elementary programming should check out our Specialty Camps. Rooted in Nature and Expedition Elachee are designed specifically for grades 4–8.
