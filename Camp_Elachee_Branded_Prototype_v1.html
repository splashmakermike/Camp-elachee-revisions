A Typical Day

Your Child's Day, Hour by Hour

No mystery. No anxiety. We want you to know exactly what's happening while your child is with us. Every day has a shape to it. Here's what that looks like.

🌊 Creek Days: Thursday and Friday Every Week

Every Thursday and Friday, campers explore Chicopee Creek. It is one of the most loved parts of the whole summer. Water stays ankle-deep in most areas. No swimming ability needed. Just good shoes and a sense of adventure.

  • Bring a towel and closed-toe water shoes
  • Wear creek outfit under clothes. No changing facilities on site.
  • No swimming ability required. All creek time is supervised.

Good to Know

Everything You Need Before You Register

We want you to feel completely prepared before your child's first day. Not scrambling for answers on a Monday morning. Here is everything you need to know right up front.

⏰ Hours & Schedule

  • Drop-off begins 8:15 AM
  • Pick-up begins 2:45 PM
  • Camp days Monday through Friday
  • Before/after care Not available at this time
  • Early pick-up Must be by 2:00 PM
  • Late pick-up Consistent late pick-up may incur a fee. We appreciate your punctuality.

👧 Who Can Attend

  • Elementary camp Must be 5 years old by Sept 1, 2026. Entering K–6th grade.
  • Specialty camps Students entering grades 4–8
  • Prior experience Not required. All skill levels welcome.
  • Swimming ability Not required for creek activities
  • Special needs Contact us to discuss options

🎒 What to Pack Every Day

  • Water bottle Labeled, easy to carry independently
  • Lunch Packed in one lunchbox your child can carry
  • Snacks At least 2. Camp burns a lot of energy!
  • Hat Optional but recommended
  • Sunscreen/bugs Provided by Elachee. Bring your own if your child has specific needs.
🥜 Nut-free environment. Please do not pack items containing nuts of any kind.

🌊 Creek Day Extras

  • Creek days Every Thursday & Friday
  • Towel Bring a full-size towel
  • Water shoes Closed-toe only
  • Wear it in Dress in creek clothes under regular clothes. No changing facilities on site.
  • Water depth Ankle-deep in most areas

What Happens After You Click Register
  • 1
    Create your account — Our registration lives on the Campsite platform. Create a simple family account on your first visit. It takes about two minutes.

    Select your week(s) and camper(s) — Browse available themes and dates. Love more than one theme? Add multiple weeks. If a session is full, join the waitlist. Spots open up regularly.
